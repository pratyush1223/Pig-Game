Instruction

This is a Pig Game 🎲. You roll a dice, if you get 1, you will lose your current score ❌. Except 1, all your current scores are added. If you do not get 1, you can hold your score any time and it is assigned to your total score. Then the second player roll a dice. Game lasts until, one player reaches 100 point 🎯.
